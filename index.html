A man is searching caves for treasure, when he's face to face with a sphinx. The sphinx tells the man: "If you can answer my riddle, I will let you leave and take all the treasure you desire. The riddle is: "

"I have seas but no water. Mountains but no land. I have towns but no people, coasts but no sand. What am I?"
As the man tried to come up with an answer, he ran out of time and was devoured by the sphinx.

the cold laughter cuts to the bone

An english professor is approached by one of his students who pleads and begs for an extension for an assignment. The professor, being clever gave him this riddle:

"What English word retains the same pronunciation, even after you take away four of its five letters?"
Racking his brain, the student went through all the five letter words he knew before he suddenely chanced on it when he started spelling one.
